Features

Word-Level Precision

Powered by OpenAI's Whisper, Merlin generates word-by-word timestamps for perfect synchronization with your video.

Customizable Styling

Choose from pre-built templates or customize fonts, colors, positioning, and opacity to match your brand perfectly.

Real-Time Preview

See your captions exactly as they'll appear in the final video. Edit words inline and preview changes instantly.

100% Local Processing

Your videos never leave your Mac. All transcription and rendering happens locally using Whisper and FFmpeg.

No Subscriptions

Free and open source. No monthly fees, no upload limits, no ongoing costs. Use it as much as you want.

Professional Quality

Export broadcast-quality videos with perfectly synchronized captions using industry-standard ASS subtitle rendering.

Built With Best-in-Class Tools

Merlin is powered by OpenAI Whisper for state-of-the-art speech recognition and FFmpeg for professional video encoding. All wrapped in a beautiful, native macOS experience.

Merlin is free and open source.
